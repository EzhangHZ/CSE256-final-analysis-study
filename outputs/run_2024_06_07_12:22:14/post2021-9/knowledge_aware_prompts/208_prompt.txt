# Knowledge-Aware Prompt:

1. Read t, the number of test cases.

2. Loop t times to read each test case.

3. Read n, the number of cards.

4. Initialize counters for white cards and black cards for Alice and Bob.

5. Iterate through the deck of cards, following the dealing pattern described for Alice and Bob.

6. Keep track of the number of cards dealt to Alice and Bob at each step, updating the counts of white and black cards accordingly.

7. After going through all steps, calculate and print the final counts of white and black cards for Alice and Bob.

8. Repeat the process for each test case.

9. End all loops.
